    Evaporative Cooling Systems

    Evaporative cooling systems, also known as swamp coolers or desert coolers, cool air by the evaporation of water. They draw in hot, dry air and pass it over wet pads, where water evaporates, absorbing heat and lowering the air's temperature. This cooled, humidified air is then circulated into the space.
